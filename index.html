about me

Hi! My name is Arushi. I am a high school student in Singapore with a passion for design, food and music. I love to read and to take photos in my free time. There are 3 things that I want to be in the future: a product designer, a pastry chef and a teacher. I am an Indian, born in Japan and living in Singapore since I was 6 years old. I have been at SJI International since Grade 3.

As a child I've always been interested in making stuff, whether it'd be a puzzle or a chocolate concoction, and at 16 years old I started to mess with Fusion 360 and CorelDraw (my favourite softwares for design).
So here I am now, showing you some of the things I've made.

Enjoy!
